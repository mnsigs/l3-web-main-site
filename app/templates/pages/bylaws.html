Sigma Chi Fraternity's Greater Minnesota Alumni Chapter Bylaws


PREAMBLE

Believing that where there is law there is liberty, we the members of the Greater Minnesota Alumni Chapter of the Sigma Chi Fraternity do hereby establish and solemnly promise to obey these bylaws to supplement and support the Ritual, Constitution, Statutes and Executive Committee Regulations of the Sigma Chi Fraternity for the government of our organization.
